About RCB

Founded in 2008, Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is one of the most passionate and followed franchises in the IPL. Known for their iconic red and gold, RCB stands for passion, loyalty, and fire on and off the field. The team has been home to cricketing legends and is celebrated for its never-give-up attitude and electrifying fan base.
